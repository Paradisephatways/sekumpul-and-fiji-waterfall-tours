Information About Waterfall Tour

Before we embark on the tour, there are a few items you should bring along to ensure a pleasant and enjoyable experience. Here's a detailed list of things to consider:

1. Swimsuits:As we'll be visiting various water attractions, having swimsuits will allow you to fully enjoy these activities.

2. Towel: Bringing a towel is essential for drying off after swimming or participating in water-based activities. It's also handy for sunbathing or lounging on the waterfall.

3. Sun protection: It's crucial to shield yourself from the sun's harmful rays. Consider bringing sunscreen with a high SPF, a wide-brimmed hat, sunglasses, and protective clothing to minimize sunburn and other sun-related risks.

4. Mosquito spray: Depending on the location and time of year, there may be mosquitoes or other insects. Carrying mosquito spray will help protect you from bites and potential discomfort.

5. Sturdy shoes: As we may be exploring different areas, comfortable and sturdy shoes are recommended. Choose closed-toe shoes that offer support and traction, as some paths or attractions may be uneven or require walking.

6. Slippers or water shoes: While sturdy shoes are recommended for walking, it's also practical to bring slippers or water shoes for activities that involve water, such as swimming or walking along the waterfall.

7. Money: Although there's no need to purchase anything during the tour, having some spare money is convenient for snacks, drinks, and souvenirs. You may come across local vendors or stalls offering unique items or local delicacies.

Short Tour

STEP 1

Start at the parking area: Begin your journey at the designated parking area near Lemukih village.

STEP 2

Trek to the waterfall (1.5km): Embark on a trek that takes you through the picturesque countryside. Enjoy the scenic views of the rice terraces and tropical fruit gardens along the way.

STEP 3

We will stop at top of waterfall, including the spring water source that feeds it. Be cautious and follow any safety instructions provided by local guides.

STEP 4

Visit Sekumpul and Fiji Waterfall: From the top of Sekumpul Waterfall, continue your trek going down with stairs to visit the nearby Fiji and Sekumpull Waterfall. Enjoy the beauty of both waterfalls and take in the tranquil atmosphere of the surroundings.

STEP 5

Stop at a warung for lunch with rice terrace view: After the waterfall exploration, take a break and enjoy a delicious lunch at a local warung (small restaurant) that offers a scenic view of the nearby rice terraces. It's a perfect opportunity to relax and refuel.

STEP 6

Return to the parking area: After lunch, start your journey back to the parking area. Follow the same path you took earlier, relishing the surrounding natural beauty once again.

Note

The entire tour, including trekking, exploring the waterfalls, lunch, and returning to the parking area, is estimated to take around 2 to 3 hours. However, the actual time can vary depending on your pace, the amount of time spent at each location, and other individual factors.
